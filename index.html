My Minecraft World

Overworld

The Overworld is the starting dimension in Minecraft. It is the most diverse and interesting dimension with many different biomes, structures, and mobs. Some notable things to find in the Overworld include:

  • Villages
  • Temples
  • Mineshafts
  • Strongholds
  • Woodland Mansions
  • Pillager Outposts
  • Nether Portals

Nether

The Nether is a dangerous dimension in Minecraft filled with lava, fire, and dangerous mobs. Despite its dangers, there are many unique resources and structures to be found in the Nether. Some notable things to find in the Nether include:

  • Nether Fortresses
  • Bastion Remnants
  • Crimson Forests
  • Warped Forests
  • Soul Sand Valleys
  • Basalt Deltas
  • Piglin Bastions

End

The End is the final dimension in Minecraft, and is only accessible by defeating the Ender Dragon in the Overworld. The End is a desolate place with little resources or structures, but it is home to the Ender Dragon and the unique block, End Stone. If you manage to defeat the Ender Dragon, you will be able to explore the outer islands of the End and find:

  • End Cities
  • End Ships
  • Chorus Trees
  • Shulker Boxes
  • Elytra Wings
